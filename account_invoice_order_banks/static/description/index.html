Client Bank Account on SO and Invoice

Add your Company bank account on sale documents!

This module adds a Company bank account to the partner.

This account is automatically set on the Sale Order and Invoice form while selecting the customer.

Finally, this modules also adds the company bank account and client bank fieldsto the invoice. If filled, the client's bank account is printed.
