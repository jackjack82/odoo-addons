POS Order Email Receipt in Odoo

This app is designed specially for those whose customers wants to have Point of sales order receipt in their mails, in place of hard copy. In between pos session if customer wants to get receipt on a different email address, seller can change or update the email address and send the receipt by email in POS Odoo.

Features

Email to customer about POS order.

Sellers are allowed to send email to the customer.

POS order receipt send to customer

Sellers are allowed to send receipt to the customer through email.

Special popup to change email address

Sellers are allowed to change the email address, as per the customer request.

Seller can update email address

Sellers are allowed to update the email address, in between a session and Customer's email address would be update in the backend.
Demo Request Video Tutorial

POS Email Receipt Button

After selecting customer and product, in POS Payment screen user have to click on the Email Receipt button, to send POS receipt to the customers.


On click, the Email Receipt button, it gets highlighted.

POS receipt will be send to the Customer as the seller validate the order.


Button to change the email of the Customer

Button allows to change the email of the customer, if he/she asks for it.


Change Email popup

Popup will appear to seller, which allows to change the email of the customer.


Email send to Customer from POS

Customer email by the seller, can be seen which includes the receipt, on the choosen email address of customer.


Update the email of the customer.

Update the email of the customer, by checking the 'Update Your Email' check box, and clicking on 'OK'. As user update the email the customer email would be updated in the backend.


Email send to Customer from POS

Customer email by the seller, can be seen which includes the receipt, on the updated email address of customer.


Customer Receipt through email by the seller.
